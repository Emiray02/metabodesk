METABODESK SETUP - IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Thank you for installing MetaboDesk.

This installer will:
- Install the MetaboDesk application
- Download required runtime files from the internet (~800 MB)
- Install Python packages automatically (memote, carveme, etc.)
- Create a Start Menu shortcut (optional desktop shortcut)

Requirements:
- Active internet connection (required for first-time install)
- Approximately 1.5 GB of free disk space
- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)

Notes:
- The download is approximately 800 MB and may take several minutes
  depending on your internet connection speed.
- Please do not close the installer while downloading.
- If the download fails, you can retry by running the installer again.
- Administrator permission may be required.
- If you already have a previous version, it will be replaced.

Click "Next" to continue.
